Colab GPU runtime on April 30, 2026.

## Result

| Model | Case | ROC-AUC | Accuracy |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| FaceNet baseline | masked-unmasked | 0.7900 | 0.7573 |
| Pair head, masked-only policy | masked-unmasked | 0.7914 | 0.7493 |
| FaceNet baseline | unmasked-unmasked | 0.9570 | 0.8996 |
| Pair head, masked-only policy | unmasked-unmasked | 0.9570 | 0.8996 |

Masked-unmasked gain: `+0.0014` ROC-AUC.

## Interpretation

The extension is a useful transfer check, but not a stronger final direction.
The LFW synthetic training root gives only a marginal masked-unmasked ROC-AUC
gain on real RMFRD evaluation identities. The final report should keep the
RMFRD-trained pair head as the main method and present this as evidence that
simple synthetic masks alone do not transfer enough to replace target-domain
pair supervision.
